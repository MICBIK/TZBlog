# site-polish spec

## SCENARIO: site-polish-001

**GIVEN** a reader lands on the homepage
**WHEN** the page renders
**THEN** it communicates TZBlog's stack, implementation direction, and editorial intent through richer sections, background depth, and reveal-ready visual hooks

## SCENARIO: site-polish-002

**GIVEN** a reader opens the about page
**WHEN** the page renders
**THEN** it explains the project architecture, self-hosted direction, and writing principles with concrete content rather than generic placeholder copy

## SCENARIO: site-polish-003

**GIVEN** launch polish is applied
**WHEN** static/global styles are inspected
**THEN** the site has reusable launch-surface primitives for subtle backgrounds, panels, and motion that preserve reduced-motion behavior
